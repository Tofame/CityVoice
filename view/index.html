Budget of Townsfolk

Thanks to the participatory budget, the residents decide how a portion of [City]’s budget is spent. Every year, [City]’s citizens come up with and submit project ideas, then vote on which ones will be brought to life.

This way, the community collectively shapes what will be built in their neighborhoods, across districts, or throughout the entire city. Any resident can propose a project or cast a vote — no official registration or adulthood required.
